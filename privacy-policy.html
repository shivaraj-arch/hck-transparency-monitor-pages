Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 May 2026

Karnataka High Court Monitor provides a mobile-friendly view of publicly published Karnataka High Court listing and hearing data.

Information We Collect

The app itself does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, phone number, address, or payment details.

Data Used by the App

  • The app reads publicly available dashboard data and JSON files published from this project.
  • The app may store limited local preferences on your device, such as display or theme settings.
  • The app does not provide its own advertising, analytics, or in-app tracking systems.

Network Services

The app loads content from GitHub-hosted pages and related public files used by the dashboard. Those services may process standard technical request data such as IP address, browser or device metadata, and access logs under their own policies.

External Links

The app may open external links such as email, issue reporting pages, or UPI payment links. When you leave the app and open another service, that service's own privacy practices apply.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed specifically to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page.

Contact
